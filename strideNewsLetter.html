March Newsletter: Streamlining Fee Payments to TREC

To streamline fee payments to the Texas Real Estate Commission (TREC), utilizing their online services portal is highly recommended for various transactions such as license applications or renewals, and business entity registrations. This method ensures both convenience and security, as the portal is accessible 24/7, 365 days a year. Here's a summary of the process:

  • Access the TREC online services portal.
  • Input necessary information and ensure to provide your email for notifications.
  • Proceed through a series of pages with questions. Confirm and certify the information provided.
  • Complete the payment process on the designated payment screen.

Additionally, a 67-second video and step-by-step visual explainers are available to guide users through the online payment process, making it easy to follow regardless of the transaction type.
